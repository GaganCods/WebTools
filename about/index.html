The Mission

In a world where everything is moving to the cloud, we noticed a dangerous trend: simple tasks requiring you to upload sensitive data to remote servers.

We asked ourselves: "Why does a password generator need an internet connection?" or "Why upload a 5MB image just to compress it?"

Our mission is to bring the power back to your device. By leveraging modern browser technologies (WebAssembly, Canvas API), we deliver desktop-class performance without the privacy risks.
