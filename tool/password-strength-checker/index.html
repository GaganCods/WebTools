How Cracking Works

Hackers don't guess passwords manually. They use vast "Rainbow Tables" (lists of common passwords) and GPU clusters to test billions of combinations per second.

The math is simple: The longer and more complex your password is, the exponentially more time it takes to compute. A 6-character password can be cracked instantly. A 16-character mixed password would take trillions of years.
